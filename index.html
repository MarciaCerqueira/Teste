The 7 Brawler Classes

⚔️
Anti-Tank
First Pick
  • Definition: High burst damage (~1 sec) and/or a stun
  • Almost always the first pick in aggressive modes
  • Never draft without one in Brawl Ball, Hot Zone, Gem Grab, Heist
  • Most well-rounded class — little to no weakness
Otis Crow Colette Chester M
🔴
Space Makers
Most Important
  • Definition: Dash/teleport that closes distance instantly
  • Takes 2 opponents to deal with them most of the time
  • Forces opponents away from snipers/throwers
  • Best in slots 4-5, not early draft
  • The gap between casuals and pros
Kaz Shade Ally Mortis Edgar
🌀
Throwers
Last Pick Only
  • Definition: Shoot over walls
  • Binary: either completely useless or totally unbeatable
  • Almost exclusively last pick — never pick early
  • Special: Willow (anti-tank properties, good 1st/2-3), Barry (healer in Heist/Hot Zone)
Sirius Larry Guju Barry Ziggy
🔵
Control
Slots 2-3
  • Definition: Good at holding map areas, never both high DPS + stun
  • Counter other mid-range brawlers, especially anti-tanks
  • Never first pick — easy to get run down
  • Fallen off significantly due to space makers dominating
Lumi Niga Gene Stew Leon
🎯
Snipers
Bounty/KO
  • Definition: Long range, single fire shots
  • Mostly for Bounty and Knockout only
  • Pure snipers (Piper, Mandy) rarely viable at high level anymore
  • Exceptions: Belle, Byron, Gus, Angelo, Pierce, RT can be played more broadly
Pierce Angelo Belle Byron Gus
🛡️
Tanks
vs Space Makers
  • Definition: Massive HP, no mobility
  • Counter Space Makers by outlasting them in 1v1s
  • Pick when opponent has Space Makers but no anti-tank
  • Still weak to: throwers, snipers, anti-tanks
  • Only class with passive supers
Bull Frank BB Fang Rosa
💚
Support
Utility
  • Definition: Heals, speed, utility — enables teammates
  • Extremely strong when uncountered
  • Weakness: low damage — always pair with damage dealers
  • Complete the comp after anti-tank is secured
Max Poco Barry Kit Glowy

04

How Bans Work

🥈 You Have Second Pick Opponent goes first
Aggressive Modes

Ban the 3 best anti-tanks on the map — Crow, Colette, Otis, Chester. No overthinking.

Passive Modes (Bounty/KO)

Ban Niga, Gene, Pierce — the best first picks for those modes.

Overtuned Brawlers

New or recently buffed brawlers like Niga and Sirius are also priority bans even outside their class.

🥇 You Have First Pick You go first
⚠️ Never Ban the Best Brawlers

When your team has first pick, never ban Crow, Colette, etc. — you want to pick those yourself!

Option 1 — Ban a Strong 2-3

Stops opponent building a solid comp. Aggressive: Stew, Lumi, Ruffs, Mortis, Edgar. Passive: Belle or Byron.

Option 2 — Ban a Strong Last Pick

Stops a free counter at the end. Examples: Rico on Hard Rock Mine, Shade on walled maps, throwers in Bounty/KO.

05

Dealing With Bad Teammates

🧠The Bigger Picture
Most Common Mistake

Only looking at your own brawler. Always look at what your teammates already counter. Don't pick a 3rd brawler that counters the same thing.

If Teammate First Picks Space Maker (e.g. Mortis)

Don't panic. They will counter Mortis → you counter the counter. Force them to hard-commit → your last pick gets a free matchup.

Never Give Up

A bad first pick doesn't mean you auto-lose. Use your draft knowledge to save the game with smart picks 2-5.

06

Key Rules to Always Remember

1
Always have an anti-tank in aggressive modes — non-negotiable.
2
Throwers and snipers are last pick in most scenarios.
3
Never first pick control — easy to get run down by tanks and space makers.
4
Space Maker + Anti-Tank combo wins almost every aggressive game.
5
Tanks counter space makers when opponent has no anti-tank.
6
Support needs damage dealers alongside them or you get run down.
7
Meta changes constantly — follow pro tier lists after every balance update.
8
New brawlers are always overtuned on release — prioritize banning/picking them.
